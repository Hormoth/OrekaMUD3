                         W E L C O M E   T O   O R E K A
================================================================================

  The world was shaped by four Elemental Lords -- Stone, Fire, Sea, and Wind.
  They breathed life into the land, raised the mountains, filled the oceans,
  and gave birth to the Kin who now walk Oreka's many roads.

  The Fall of Aldenheim changed everything. From that ruin rose the Ascended
  Gods -- mortal Kin who burned so brightly in sacrifice that the world itself
  remembered them. Their stories are told in every hearth-fire, every tide.

  Now it is your turn.

  Oreka is a world of elemental concord and ancient conflict. Ruins hold
  secrets older than memory. Factions compete for power, faith, and survival.
  The road ahead is yours to walk -- carefully.

  Type 'help' at any time for a list of commands.
  Type 'score' to see your character sheet.
