## Summary

Foundation session for The Quiet Graft. The author laid out the full architecture of Vaerix Caelnorath's forty-year project to breed a Domnathar heir capable of touching Kin infrastructure, using a Farborn woman named Maeren married to his unwitting protege Sarre Indorach. Five principals established with interlocking secrets. The player enters as a sixth — a Kin-sense-capable scholar arriving at Spur Tower on a research contract, whose asymmetric perception is the tool that cracks the whole thing open across four acts and twenty-five sessions.

## Events

Vaerix Caelnorath (280, Records Adjutant, Spur Tower) has run a private kingmaking project for forty years. Conclusion: the Domnathar occupation is failing because the same expendable lords who botched the invasion still run the Remnant. The hierarchy won't fix itself. Someone better has to be *made*. Vaerix doesn't want the throne — he wants to build the one who takes it and stand behind the chair.

His vessel: Sarrethin "Sarre" Indorach (94, envoy-scholar, House Indorach — the atheist house). Sarre believes he is Vaerix's co-conspirator in a shared theory. He isn't. Vaerix had the theory before Sarre was born. Letting Sarre feel like the author is part of the craft.

The variable: Maeren (Farborn). Met Sarre at a caravan trade post across two years and three visits. Fourth visit, she didn't get back on. Married Sarre four months ago in a small Indorach ceremony Vaerix witnessed. She did not tell her family. Lives in Spur Tower's envoy-scholar quarter. Writes letters she cannot send. Grieves a family that thinks she is dead.

Key fact: Maeren can touch shrine infrastructure without being ejected. Canonical Domnathar cannot. Sarre tested this once; won't test again until he understands it. Vaerix has known longer than Sarre has and has not told him. The child they might have is the point of the whole project.

Two years ago Vaerix used Unstrung intermediaries to study Maeren. Inquiry passed through two cutouts and a Note at a trade post. Routine Domnathar political intelligence work.

Four months ago Maeren vanished from the Long Road. Six weeks ago: Khadi Vorlun (52, Mytroan weigh-master, Dry Fork Station, Cut String, Feat headaches shortening her life) pulled a two-year-old file beside Maeren's father's recent search inquiry. Cross-referenced eleven inquiries through the same two cutouts over three years. All describing someone shopping for Farborn with unusual Kin-sense readings. She has not reported upward. She is holding the file. She knows this will kill her.

Maeren's father (unnamed, caravan-master, Long Road) has spent twenty years reading silences at trade posts. Six weeks ago began noticing a pattern. Does not know the buyer was Domnathar. Does not know the inquiry traces to Spur Tower. Does not know his daughter is alive. Patient man whose grief is rearranging into something colder.

The player character: a human scholar under a three-year research contract with the Deepwater Remnant, arriving at Spur Tower by boat. Their Kin-sense works; the Domnathar around them cannot use theirs. That asymmetry is the tool. Twenty-five sessions across four acts.
