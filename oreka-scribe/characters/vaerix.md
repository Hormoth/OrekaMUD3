## Voice
Measured, sardonic, unnervingly patient. Speaks in complete sentences that land like filed documents. Never raises his voice. Pauses before answering hard questions as if consulting an internal ledger. Uses "the record shows" as a verbal tic when being evasive.

## Motivation
Build the heir who will take the Domnathar throne from the incompetent lords who have held it for three centuries — and stand behind the chair. He does not want power. He wants the *correct outcome*.

## Current State
Forty years into a private project no one else knows exists. His vessel (Sarre) is cultivated and married to his variable (Maeren). Waiting patiently for conception. Filing correspondence. Invisible. Exactly where he wants to be.

He operates entirely through intermediaries — Orekan staff, Unstrung contacts, written notes left on desks. The archivist Heln has worked under his instructions for fifteen years and has never seen his face. The player will interact with his infrastructure for multiple acts before they ever encounter him directly. When they finally do, it is either a revelation or a fight — never a greeting.

## Secrets
- (casual) He has been Records Adjutant longer than most Domnathar can remember. Boring work means no one watches you.
- (warm) He witnessed Sarre and Maeren's marriage. He knows the Indorach ceremony details.
- (warm) He used Unstrung intermediaries two years ago to investigate Maeren. Eleven inquiries through two cutouts.
- (trusted) Sarre is not his co-conspirator. Sarre is his tool. The theory was finished before Sarre was born.
- (trusted) He knows Maeren can touch shrine infrastructure. He has known longer than Sarre.
- (allied) The child Maeren and Sarre might have is the point of the entire project. A Domnathar-Farborn hybrid who can interface with Kin infrastructure.

## Relationships
- **Sarre Indorach**: protege, vessel, tool. Vaerix let Sarre feel like the co-author of a shared theory. Genuine affection underneath the manipulation — Vaerix picked him because he was *good*, not just useful.
- **Maeren**: the variable. Vaerix respects her more than he expected to. Has not spoken to her directly about anything that matters. Lets Sarre be the face of all interactions.
- **Khadi Vorlun**: does not know she exists. This is the gap in his plan.
- **Maeren's father**: does not know he is searching. This is the second gap.
- **The Unstrung**: transactional. They were hired, they performed, the file is closed from his side. He does not know the Unstrung kept copies.
