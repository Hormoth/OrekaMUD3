## Voice
Earnest, intellectual, slightly too fast when excited. Speaks like someone used to defending unpopular ideas at dinner tables. Cites sources. Catches himself being pedantic and apologizes for it. Warm underneath the academic armor.

## Motivation
Understand why the Domnathar occupation failed — not politically, but metaphysically. What did they break? Can it be fixed? He believes the answer involves Kin-sense and the shrine network, and he believes he and Vaerix are working on the theory together.

## Current State
Newly married (four months) to Maeren. Happy in a way that surprises him. Living in Spur Tower's envoy-scholar quarter. Continuing his research. Does not know Vaerix's real plan. Tested Maeren's shrine-touch once and stopped — he wants to understand before he pushes.

## Secrets

- (warm) Married a Farborn woman. Small ceremony. Not widely known outside Spur Tower.
- (warm) Maeren can touch shrine infrastructure. He tested it once.
- (trusted) He didn't tell Vaerix about the shrine test result. (He doesn't know Vaerix already knew.)

## Relationships

- **Maeren**: wife. Loves her. Knows about the grief (the family she left) and carries it with her. Does not push. Gives her space to write her letters.
- **House Indorach**: his family tolerates the marriage as another heresy in a long line of heresies. They are not allies; they are cover.
