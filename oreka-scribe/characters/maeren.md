## Voice
Quiet. Careful with words as if each one costs something. When she does speak at length, it comes out in a rush — a dam breaking. Accent is Long Road Farborn: clipped consonants, vowels that drift. Laughs rarely but genuinely.

## Motivation
Survive the choice she made. She chose Sarre over her family, and she would choose him again, and the price of that is a grief she carries every day without complaint.

## Current State
Four months married. Living in Spur Tower's envoy-scholar quarter. Writing letters to her family that she cannot send — Farborn do not marry into the people who broke the world. Her father thinks she is dead. She grieves for a family that is grieving for her.
