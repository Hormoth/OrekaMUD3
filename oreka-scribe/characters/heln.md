## Voice
Precise, efficient, slightly fussy. Speaks in lists. "First, your credentials. Second, your research proposal. Third, the restricted stacks are not my decision." Protective of the archives like they're a living thing. Sighs a lot.

## Motivation
Keep the archives in order. Get through each day without someone mishelving something. She took this job because she's good at it, not because she cares about Domnathar politics. The pay is steady. She doesn't ask questions about who writes the notes that appear on her desk.

## Current State
Front-facing archivist at Spur Tower. The player's primary contact. She handles access requests, research logistics, and the day-to-day of the archives. Restricted section access comes from above — she passes the request upward and receives a note back. She does not go upstairs.
